Burritos

Burritos are my favorite food largely due to their convenience and their versatility. Many things can be put into a burrito,
and the most common inclusions (rice, beans, chicken, etc.) are affordable while being quite delicious. One of the best dishes
that my mom makes is a burrito, and I remember always being happy when she made them.

Ingredients

You can put anything in a burrito, but below are the things I most prefer to put in mine, inside of a corn tortilla:

Black beans
Brown rice
Guacamole
Grilled chicken
Chili powder
Cumin
