ASCII to International Phonetic Alphabet Converter

This page allows you to convert a custom ASCII writing system called TIPO (Typeable International Phonetic Orthography) into usable IPA (International Phonetic Alphabet) text. Using TIPO to transcribe the IPA is far more convenient than manual IPA transcription, since TIPO was designed to only use printable ASCII characters.
